about me

hi! i am andrew lin, a third-year undergraduate student at the university of chicago. i major in sociology and media arts and design, and i am very passionate about games and play. i seek to both examine them as cultural objects, and try to incorporate my own reflections of sociology into my design process.

i have mostly worked on team projects and i really value coordination and communication in the game design process. i am also eager to apply my academic learnings, especially my study of the sociology of culture, in real-world contexts.

please feel free to browse through my portfolio to see my projects, accomplishments, and the skills i bring to the table!
